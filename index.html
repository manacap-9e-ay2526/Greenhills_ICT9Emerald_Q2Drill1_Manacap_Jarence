Emergency Go Bag

What to pack in Your Emergency Kit

FOOD AND WATER

Food and Water are essential for humans to survive. Although it depends on the person, one can usually survive 1 month without food but only 1 week without water.

Medkit

The medical kit is a compact collection of essential supplies designed for providing immediate care during injuries, illnesses, or emergencies. It typically contains bandages, sterile gauze, adhesive tape, antiseptic wipes, scissors, tweezers, disposable gloves, pain relievers, and basic medications.

Hygeine Kit

A hygiene kit is a small set of items used to maintain cleanliness and personal care. It usually includes soap, a toothbrush, toothpaste, shampoo, a comb, tissues, and a small towel. Some kits may also contain hand sanitizer, wet wipes, and deodorant. The purpose of a hygiene kit is to help individuals stay clean, healthy, and comfortable in daily life or during travel and emergencies.

Contact List

A contact list is an organized record of important people and their information. It usually includes names, phone numbers, email addresses, and any other details needed for communication. A contact list helps individuals reach family members, friends, teachers, classmates, or emergency services quickly. It is useful for personal use, school purposes, and emergency situations where fast communication is required.
